## Venue and Travel

The event will be held in **room 237B in the Advanced Research Centre** at the University of Glasgow's main Gilmorehill campus, in the West End of Glasgow. The venue is a short walk from Hillhead subway station. The subway, in turn, is easily accessible from Glasgow Central and Glasgow Queen Street railway stations, and Buchanan Street bus station. If arriving by plane, there are regular buses to Buchanan Street bus station from both Glasgow International Airport and Edinburgh International Airport. For further details, see [The University's travel pages](https://www.gla.ac.uk/explore/maps/).
